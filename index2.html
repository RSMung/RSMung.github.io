Short Biography

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the College of Computer Science, Chong Qing University(CQU) advised by Prof. Yantao Li and Prof. Huafeng Qin. I received my bachelor's degree from Wuhan University of Technology(WHUT) in 2020. I was a Master student from September 2020 to June 2022, and then transferred to the PhD program in September 2022.
